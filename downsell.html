Scenario: After-hours plumbing emergency

Generic gets the job done. Personalised feels like you can actually help.

Before

Hi, sorry we missed your call. Please reply with your name, what you need help with, and the best time to call you back.

After

Sorry we missed your call, we are likely on another plumbing job right now. If this is urgent, reply URGENT with your postcode and the issue. If it is not urgent, send your name, postcode, and a quick note on the job and we will get back to you as soon as we are free.

The personalised version sounds local, relevant, and in-control. It tells the lead exactly what to do next and makes an urgent job feel understood straight away.

Scenario: Quote follow-up / price shopper

One sounds like a template. The other sounds like a business ready to move.

Before

Hi, sorry we missed your call. Reply with a few details about the job and we will get back to you soon.

After

Sorry we missed your call. If you are looking for a quote, reply with the job type, postcode, and any photos if you have them. If you already have a quote from us, reply QUOTE and we will chase it up faster.

This version filters the situation fast, gives the lead a simpler action, and sounds far less generic than a catch-all missed-call text.
