# Landing Page Style Guide - AI Guide Funnels

## Design Goal
Professional, trustworthy, practical.

Should feel like a business resource or book, not a tech startup product.

Target psychology:
Small business owners prefer clear, calm, authoritative design.

Avoid futuristic AI aesthetics.

## Colour Scheme

Primary Background
`#1F2A44`
Deep navy - trust, professionalism.

Accent
`#C9A86A`
Muted gold - use sparingly for:
- the word AI in headlines
- separators
- badges

Body Background
`#F5F3EE`
Warm off-white - editorial/book feel.

CTA Button
`#2E5FA4`

CTA Hover
`#244A82`

Avoid bright colours (orange / neon / tech gradients).

## Typography

Headline Font
`Playfair Display`

Body Font
`Inter`

Section Headers
`Inter Semi Bold`

## Typography Usage

Headline Example
"Finally understand how AI fits into your business"

Highlight `AI` in gold (`#C9A86A`).

Section Headers
All caps works well.

Example
`INSIDE THE GUIDE`

## Hero Section Structure

Tag
`FOR SMALL BUSINESS OWNERS`

Headline
`Finally Understand How to Use AI in Your Small Business`

Subheadline
`A simple, practical guide showing small business owners how to use AI to save time, create marketing, and handle everyday business tasks faster.`

Book Image

CTA
`Get the Guide`

Sub-text
`Instant download · One-time purchase`

Optional Outcome Bullets
- Write marketing in seconds
- Handle emails and planning faster
- Save hours every week

## Visual Style

Minimal graphics.

Preferred aesthetic:
- business book
- consulting resource
- professional guide

Not:
- startup SaaS page
- futuristic AI product
- tech landing page

## Design References

Think:
- Harvard Business Review
- McKinsey reports
- professional business books

Not:
- AI startup websites
- SaaS dashboards
- glowing circuit imagery

## Things to Avoid

- No neon tech gradients
- No glowing AI brains
- No circuit board graphics
- No overly colourful icons
- No startup design patterns

Goal: boring but trustworthy.
